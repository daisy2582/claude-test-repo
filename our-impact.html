Your Impact

What One Person Saves Per Year

By choosing a plant-based diet, a single person saves

🐾

200+ Animal Lives

Including cows, pigs, chickens, turkeys, fish, and other marine animals. Each life represents a sentient being who wanted to live.

💧

401,500 Gallons of Water

Enough water to fill 15 swimming pools. Animal agriculture is the most water-intensive industry on the planet.

🌳

7,300 lbs of CO2

Equivalent to not driving for 8 months. A plant-based diet is the single most effective personal action for fighting climate change.

🌾

14,600 lbs of Grain

Grain that could feed hungry people instead of being funneled through the inefficient process of animal agriculture.

🌍

10,950 sq ft of Forest

Forest that would otherwise be cleared for grazing or growing animal feed crops. Preserving forests protects biodiversity and fights climate change.

❤️

Your Own Health

Reduced risk of heart disease, type 2 diabetes, certain cancers, and obesity. A well-planned vegan diet is one of the healthiest ways to eat.
