Industries

Behind Closed Doors

🐄

Factory Farming

Animals are confined in cramped spaces, unable to move freely. Cows, pigs, and chickens suffer in industrial operations designed for maximum profit at the expense of welfare.

🥛

The Dairy Industry

Dairy cows are repeatedly impregnated and separated from their calves. They produce 10x more milk than natural, leading to painful conditions. Most are slaughtered after just 4-5 years.

🥚

The Egg Industry

Hens are crammed into battery cages with less space than a sheet of paper. Male chicks are killed on their first day of life. Even "free range" often means thousands of birds in a single shed.

🐟

Commercial Fishing

Trillions of fish are caught annually, suffering slow deaths. Bycatch kills millions of dolphins, turtles, and sharks. Fish farms create crowded, disease-ridden conditions.
