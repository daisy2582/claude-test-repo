Dominion (2018)

A groundbreaking documentary using drone and hidden camera footage to expose the dark side of modern animal agriculture across multiple industries.

Watch Free Online →
🎬

Earthlings (2005)

Narrated by Joaquin Phoenix, this powerful documentary covers humanity's dependence on animals for food, clothing, entertainment, and scientific research.

Watch Free Online →
🎬

The Game Changers (2018)

A riveting documentary following elite athletes, special ops soldiers, and scientists who show how plant-based eating fuels peak performance.

Stream on Netflix →
🎬

Cowspiracy (2014)

Explores the devastating environmental impact of animal agriculture and why the world's leading environmental organizations are afraid to talk about it.

Stream on Netflix →
🎬

What the Health (2017)

An investigative documentary that uncovers the collusion between the health industry, pharmaceutical companies, and the animal agriculture industry.

Stream on Netflix →

Animal Liberation by Peter Singer

The foundational text of the modern animal rights movement. Singer's philosophical arguments against speciesism changed how millions think about animal ethics.

Find at Your Library →
📚

Eating Animals by Jonathan Safran Foer

A deeply personal and meticulously researched exploration of what it means to eat animals in an age of industrial farming. Equal parts memoir and investigation.

Find at Your Library →
📚

How Not to Die by Dr. Michael Greger

Evidence-based guide to the foods that can prevent and reverse the leading causes of death, backed by the latest peer-reviewed nutritional science.

Find at Your Library →
📚

Why We Love Dogs, Eat Pigs, and Wear Cows by Melanie Joy

Introduces the concept of "carnism" — the invisible belief system that conditions us to eat certain animals while loving others. A paradigm-shifting read.

Find at Your Library →
