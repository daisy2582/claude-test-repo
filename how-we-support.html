Our Commitment

How Vegan Bites
Supports Animals

We are more than a recipe platform. Every recipe shared, every community member gained, fuels our mission to end animal suffering and build a compassionate food system.

What We Do

Our Initiatives

How we put our values into action every single day

🏠

Sanctuary Partnerships

We partner with animal sanctuaries across the country, providing direct funding for rescued farm animals. Our partnerships support over 15 sanctuaries that together care for more than 2,000 rescued animals — cows, pigs, chickens, goats, and sheep who were saved from slaughter.

💰

Donation Programs

10% of all revenue from our premium content goes directly to animal welfare organizations. We have donated over $250,000 to date, supporting legal advocacy, rescue operations, and undercover investigations that expose cruelty in factory farms.

📚

Education Programs

Our free educational workshops reach over 5,000 people monthly. We run plant-based cooking classes in schools, community centers, and online — making vegan cooking accessible and approachable for everyone, regardless of experience level.

✅

Cruelty-Free Certification

Every recipe on our platform is verified cruelty-free. We work with ingredient suppliers to ensure our recommended products are 100% plant-based and ethically sourced. Our certification badge helps consumers make compassionate choices.

📢

Advocacy & Lobbying

We support legislative efforts to ban factory farming practices like battery cages, gestation crates, and forced molting. Our advocacy team works with lawmakers and signs onto amicus briefs supporting animal protection laws.

🤝

Community Building

Our online community of 50,000+ members supports each other through plant-based transitions. We host monthly challenges, recipe swaps, and mentorship programs connecting experienced vegans with newcomers.

Our Journey

Milestones & Impact

A timeline of our growing impact since we started

2020

Vegan Bites Founded

Started as a small blog with 10 recipes and a dream to make plant-based cooking accessible to everyone. Our founder's journey from animal agriculture awareness to vegan cooking inspired it all.

2021

First Sanctuary Partnership

Partnered with Gentle Barn Sanctuary, our first official sanctuary partner. Began monthly donations and organized volunteer days for our community members.

2022

10,000 Community Members

Reached 10,000 registered community members. Launched our education program with free cooking workshops in 12 cities. Donated $50,000 to animal welfare organizations.

2023

School Partnership Program

Launched plant-based cooking curriculum in 25 schools across 5 states. Partnered with 8 additional sanctuaries. Our advocacy efforts helped pass 3 local animal protection ordinances.

2024

$250,000 Total Donated

Crossed the $250,000 milestone in total donations. Expanded to 15 sanctuary partnerships. Launched our cruelty-free certification program for food brands.

2025

50,000 Members Strong

Grew to 50,000 community members worldwide. Published our 500th recipe. Began international partnerships with sanctuaries in the UK, Australia, and India.

2026

The Mission Continues

Launching our documentary series, expanding school programs to 100 schools, and setting a goal of $1 million in total donations by end of year. Every recipe shared brings us closer to a kinder world.
