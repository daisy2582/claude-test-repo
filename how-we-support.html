💚 Our Commitment

How Vegan Bites Supports Animal Welfare

Our journey of compassion -- from kitchen to community, making real change for animals every day.

Our Journey

Milestones & Initiatives

Key moments in our commitment to animal welfare

2020

Vegan Bites Founded

Started as a small recipe blog with a mission to make plant-based cooking accessible to everyone, regardless of cooking skill level.

2021

First Sanctuary Partnership

Partnered with Green Meadow Animal Sanctuary, donating 5% of all merchandise sales to support rescued farm animals.

2022

Education Program Launch

Launched "Plant-Based 101" free workshop series in schools and community centers, reaching over 5,000 students.

2023

Cruelty-Free Certification

Became certified by the Leaping Bunny program. All products and partnerships verified cruelty-free.

2024

$100K Donated

Reached our milestone of $100,000 in total donations to animal welfare organizations across 12 countries.

2025

Community Garden Program

Established 15 community gardens providing free fresh produce to underserved neighborhoods, promoting plant-based nutrition.

2026

1 Million Meals Milestone

Our recipes have inspired over 1 million plant-based meals worldwide, saving an estimated 500,000 animals from factory farming.

Our Partners

Organizations We Support

Working together for a kinder world

🏡

Green Meadow Sanctuary

Home to 200+ rescued farm animals. We fund veterinary care and daily operations through monthly donations.

🌍

World Animal Protection

Global organization working to end factory farming. We support their campaigns for better animal welfare laws.

🐾

Mercy For Animals

Undercover investigations and corporate campaigns. We amplify their message through our platform.

🌱

The Humane League

Fighting to end the abuse of animals raised for food through institutional and corporate campaigns.

📚

Food Empowerment Project

Addressing food justice issues including access to healthy foods and ethical treatment of farm workers and animals.

🐋

Sea Shepherd

Protecting marine wildlife and ocean ecosystems. We contribute to their anti-poaching and conservation efforts.
